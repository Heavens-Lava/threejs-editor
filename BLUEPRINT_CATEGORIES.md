## Usage Guidelines

### For Beginners
Start with categories 1-3 (Movement, Interaction, Combat) as these form the foundation of most projects.

### For Web Developers
Focus on categories 9 (Website Integration) and 12 (Industry-Specific) for creating enhanced websites.

### For Game Developers
Categories 1-7 cover comprehensive game development needs.

### For Advanced Users
Categories 10-11 provide utilities and specialized systems for complex projects.

---

## Blueprint Naming Convention

All blueprints follow this naming pattern:
`[Category]_[Subcategory]_[BlueprintName]_[Variant]`

Example:
- `Movement_Character_FirstPerson_Standard`
- `Combat_Weapon_RangedGun_Pistol`
- `Web_Scroll_ParallaxEffect_3D`
